Card Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are cards. By clicking on a card you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each card is hiden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each of the card will change.
